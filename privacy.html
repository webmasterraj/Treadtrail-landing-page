Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

TreadTrail collects minimal personal information:

  • Email address (for account management)
  • First name and last name (to personalize your experience)

While TreadTrail is a fitness app, we do not collect health metrics, fitness data, location information, or biometric data. Your workout data (including run statistics, completed workouts, and personal settings) is stored in our secure database and associated with your account.

Workout and Activity Data

TreadTrail collects and stores data related to your workout activities. The extent of data collection depends on whether you are using the free version or are a premium member.

For All Users:
  • Workout completion records
  • Workout duration and distance
  • Basic pace metrics
  • Workout frequency
  • Workout dates in calendar format
For Premium Members:

Premium members' data includes all of the above data collected for free users, plus the following additional data:

  • Detailed pace and speed analytics
  • Incline settings and patterns
  • Advanced workout statistics
  • Calories burned (as calculated by the app)
  • Personal records and achievement tracking
  • Custom workout settings and preferences
  • Historical performance trends
  • Detailed interval breakdowns

This workout data is associated with your account and stored in our secure database. It is used to:

  • Track your progress over time
  • Generate statistics and insights about your running habits
  • Provide personalized workout recommendations (premium feature)
  • Enhance your overall experience with the app

Your workout data is not shared with third parties or used for advertising purposes. If you delete your account, all associated workout data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

Usage Data

TreadTrail automatically collects certain technical and behavioral data when you use the app.

This Usage Data includes:

  • Device information: Device model, iOS version, screen size, storage availability
  • Technical information: IP address, device identifiers, time zone settings
  • App interaction data: App open frequency, feature usage, navigation patterns, session duration, last active session
  • Performance metrics: App load times, response times, crash reports, error logs
  • Feature engagement: Which workout programs are used most frequently, interface preferences

This usage data helps us understand how users interact with TreadTrail, identify technical issues, optimize performance, and make informed decisions about app improvements.

Crash Reporting

TreadTrail uses a third-party crash reporting service to help identify and fix technical problems. When a crash occurs, the service collects:

  • Technical information about the crash
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Actions leading up to the crash

The crash reporting service does not collect personal identifiable information such as your name or email address. The crash data is used solely for app improvement purposes and troubleshooting.
